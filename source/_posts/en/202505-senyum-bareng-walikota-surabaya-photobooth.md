An interactive photobooth where visitors can take photos with a 3D character of Cak Eri as the public figure serving as Mayor of Surabaya, equipped with a paid photo-printing system using the Midtrans payment gateway.

<!-- more -->

Museum Dr. Soetomo Surabaya presented a different way to learn history through the Cross Musea exhibition titled "Arutala", which means "high aspirations" in Sanskrit, and was open to the public from May 21-31, 2025.

In this annual exhibition, I contributed to the development of an interactive photobooth titled "Senyum Bareng Walikota Surabaya". As the title suggests, this media experience lets visitors take photos with Cak Eri, the public figure currently serving as Mayor of Surabaya, presented as a cute 3D character.

After taking a photo, visitors can download the result for free or choose to print it through a QRIS payment system integrated with the Midtrans API, a trusted payment gateway provider with broad support for banks and digital wallets commonly used in Indonesia.
