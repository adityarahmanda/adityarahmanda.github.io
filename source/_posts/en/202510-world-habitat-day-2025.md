An immersive room installation themed around a clean city without waste for the future. Powered by Hokuyo LiDAR sensors, the room's wall was transformed into an interactive touch surface that can summon fluttering butterfly particles.

<!-- more -->

On Monday, October 27, 2025, the Ministry of Public Works (Kemenpu) held the main event for World Habitat Day and World Cities Day 2025 at Sekolah Rakyat Menengah Atas (SRMA) 13 Sentra Pangudi Luhur, Bekasi City.

For this event, I contributed to the development of an interactive application for an immersive photo-spot room projected with visuals themed "Clean City Without Waste for the Future".

Developed with Unity and powered by Hokuyo LiDAR sensors, the room's wall was transformed into an interactive touch surface that can summon fluttering butterfly particles, creating a gentle visual experience and an attractive photo spot.
