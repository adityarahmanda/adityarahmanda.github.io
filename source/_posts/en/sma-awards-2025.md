An immersive tunnel installation that creates an interactive experience where visitors can submit personal messages to be displayed directly in the tunnel's projected visuals, designed specifically for SMA Awards 2025.

<!-- more -->

On Thursday, October 23, 2025, the SMA Award 2025 Awarding Night was held at Dyandra Convention Hall Surabaya, marking the culmination of this annual competition for high school students across East Java.

For this occasion, I was responsible for creating an interactive tunnel installation designed as the entrance to the event's main hall, as well as a place where visitors could share their impressions of the overall SMA Awards 2025 event.

When visitors enter the tunnel, they are presented with immersive visuals and a QR code that opens a web form where they can leave their impressions.

After the form is filled out and submitted, their selfie photo and message immediately appear in the tunnel visuals, creating a personal moment that passing visitors can see.
