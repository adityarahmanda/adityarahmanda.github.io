An interactive installation where visitors can draw or write a message on a podium and display it on the room wall, designed specifically for Paragon Beauty Science Tech 2026.

<!-- more -->

An immersive and interactive installation created for Paragon Beauty Science Tech 2026, held from
January 21–25, 2026. 

For this project, I developed an interactive drawing experience that encouraged visitors to contribute their
creativity and messages to a shared digital canvas. Using a drawing podium, participants could create
illustrations or write personal messages, which were then submitted to the immersive room environment.
Once submitted, a butterfly animation would appear and carry the drawing across the space before
placing it onto the room's digital wall
