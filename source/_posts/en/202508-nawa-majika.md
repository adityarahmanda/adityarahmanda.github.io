An immersive room installation that draws visitors into a magical world while they play by throwing balls to interact with visual content projected on the walls.

<!-- more -->

Starting in August 2025, NAWA Experience collaborated with Museum Bank Mandiri to open an immersive experience attraction titled "NAWA: Majika". The attraction was designed to enchant visitors and immerse them in a fantasy world filled with magical powers, spirits, and fairies.

At one point in the immersive journey, visitors are invited to enter a room where almost every side is projected with eye-catching visual content.

Meanwhile, the room's floor is filled with plastic balls that not only create a ball-pit play area, but also function as interactive media that trigger direct interactions with the projected visuals when thrown at the walls.

The attraction's visual content is presented through a narrated storyline divided into nine stages. Among those stages, I was responsible for developing two interactive stages.

Here is a detailed explanation of those two interactive stages.

<b>Lantern Stage</b>, in this session, magical lanterns appear on the walls. Throwing a ball at a lantern makes it glow brighter. Each brightness level has a different lantern visual, creating a satisfying moment for visitors as they light them up.
