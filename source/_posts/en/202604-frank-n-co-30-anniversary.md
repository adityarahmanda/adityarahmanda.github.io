An interactive installation where visitors can interact with deer mascots, open jewelry boxes to reveal featured products, and submit impression messages for Frank & co.'s 30th anniversary.

<!-- more -->

A large-scale immersive and interactive installation created to celebrate Frank & co.'s 30th anniversary,
held at the AA Maramis Building, Jakarta, from April 23 26. 

For this project, I was responsible for developing the interactive experiences within the Tunnel Area and
the "Future of Love" room.

The Tunnel Area featured a series of touch-based interactions, where visitors could trigger animated
responses from Frank & co.'s signature deer mascots and open virtual jewelry boxes to discover featured
collections and anniversary messages.

In the "Future of Love" room, visitors were invited to leave their wishes and messages for Frank & co.'s
30th anniversary. By scanning a QR code, guests could take a selfie, submit a personalized message, and
see their contributions displayed on a large interactive message wall, transforming the installation into a
collective celebration of love and memories.
