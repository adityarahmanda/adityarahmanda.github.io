An immersive tunnel installation that creates an interactive experience where visitors can submit personal messages to be displayed directly in the tunnel's projected visuals, designed specifically for BSI International Expo 2025.

<!-- more -->

This year, PT Bank Syariah Indonesia Tbk (BSI) once again held its annual signature event, BSI International Expo 2025, as a platform to strengthen halal literacy while reinforcing Indonesia's position in the global Islamic economy ecosystem.

For this project, I was responsible for creating an interactive experience titled "Share Your Dream" inside the tunnel connecting two large halls of the exhibition venue.

The installation was developed by combining a Unity application for the tunnel visuals with a web-based form system where visitors could enter their name, selfie photo, and dream message.

The interactive experience begins when visitors scan a QR code to open the web form. After the form is filled out and submitted, their dream message appears in the projected visuals inside the tunnel, creating a personal moment that other visitors can see.
