An interactive kiosk for the Static Diorama Room at Museum 10 Nopember Surabaya, allowing visitors to watch video content related to Surabaya's history and understand the context of the displayed dioramas.

<!-- more -->

In this project, I had the opportunity to develop an interactive kiosk for the Static Diorama Room at Museum 10 Nopember Surabaya. Through this kiosk, visitors can watch video content related to Surabaya's history and understand the context of the dioramas displayed in the room.

This interactive kiosk was developed as a major update to an older application that was already <i>out-of-maintenance</i>. The update introduced a fully redesigned <i>User Interface (UI)</i> for a more appealing look, along with a new <i>User Experience (UX)</i> designed to be more practical and intuitive.

The system update also includes language localization features to improve accessibility for international visitors.
